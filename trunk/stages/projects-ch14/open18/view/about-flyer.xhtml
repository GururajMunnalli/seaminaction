#{messages['application.title']} ...#{messages['application.tagline']} Open 18 is a golf course directory and community site for golf players. Golf course directory: The golf course directory allows you to browse for golf facilities/courses or search for one in your area. The information available includes the facility, the course, the holes, the tee sets and the tees. The clubhouse: The clubhouse is a social utility that allows you to connect with other golfers. When you register to become a golfer, you will get a profile, which you can share with others. Along with that profile, you can track your golf rounds and publish personal experiences.
